For pardon of the multitude of transgressions 
be Your divine blood and Your pure body, 
O Lover of humanity, and for communion 
with the Holy Spirit and eternal life, 
and complete estrangement from passions troubling me.

For pardon of the multitude of transgressions 
be Your divine blood and Your pure body, 
communion with Your good and all-holy Spirit, 
and eternal life, O benevolent Lord, 
and complete estrangement from passions troubling me.

For pardon of the multitude of transgressions, 
communion with the life-giving Holy Spirit, 
eternal life, and freedom from all afflictions 
and passions troubling me be Your immaculate 
body and divine blood, O benevolent Lord.

be Your most pure body 
and divine blood
